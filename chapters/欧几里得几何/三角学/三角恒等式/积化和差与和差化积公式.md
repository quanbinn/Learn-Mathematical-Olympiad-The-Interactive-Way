# 积化和差与和差化积公式

## 实验步骤

1. 令y，z为两段弧长，依据两角和与差的正弦公式，得出：

$$
\left\\{ 
    \begin{array}{c}
        sin(y+z) = sin ycos z + cos ysin z......①\\\\ 
        sin(y-z) = sin ycos z - cos ysin z......②\\\\ 
    \end{array}
\right. 
$$



$$
\left\\{ 
    \begin{array}{c}
        sin(y+z) = sin ycos z + cos ysin z &\text{Given} \tag 1\\\\ 
    \end{array}
\right. 
$$

$$ OD=EA+AC $$

$$ => sin α cos β= cos α sin β + sin(α-β) $$

$$ => sin(α-β)= sin α cos β - cos α sin β $$

$$ OC=DB+BE $$ 

$$ => cos(α-β) = cos α cos β + sin α sin β $$
